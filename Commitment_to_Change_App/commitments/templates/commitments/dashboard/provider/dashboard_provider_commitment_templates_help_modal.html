{% extends "commitments/common/base_help_modal.html" %} {% block modal_title %} Commitment Templates {% endblock modal_title %} {% block icon_color %} white {% endblock icon_color %} {% block modal_body_text %} Commitment Templates allow students in your courses to commit to a common, course-related goal. You can create as many templates as you like, and use the same commitment across multiple courses if desired.
  • Creating a Commitment Template: Click on the "Create a Commitment Template" button in the navbar at the top of your screen, and fill out the Title and Description
  • Associating a Commitment Template with your course: Once you have created a Commitment Template, you can "suggest" the commitment to students enrolled in your course by navigating to that course's page from the "Courses" section above, and clicking the "Select Templates" button. Enrolled students can then click on "Make This Commitment" button in the course page to "join" that commitment.
