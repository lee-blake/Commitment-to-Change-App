  • Creating a new Commitment: To create a new commitment to show in your dashboard, click "Make a commitment" in the navbar at the top of the screen, or use the "Make This Commitment" button in a joined course to make that course's commitment.
  • Viewing a Commitment: To view a created commitment, click on the button containing your commitment's name.
  • Completing a Commitment: Once you have finished a commitment, you can use the "Complete" button to mark it as such. It will then be moved to the "Completed Commitments" section in the "Inactive Commitments" panel below.
  • Discontinuing a Commitment: If you are unable to complete a commitment for any reason, you can use the "Discontinue" button to move it to the "Discontinued Commitments" section in the "Inactive Commitments" panel below.
